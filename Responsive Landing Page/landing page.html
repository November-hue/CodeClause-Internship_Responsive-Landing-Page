Features

Step 1: Project Inception

During the Inception phase, the November Tech team dedicates itself to thoroughly uncovering everything about your organization—from core values to short and long-term objectives. We believe in deeply understanding your business before embarking on crafting your digital footprint. This discovery process hinges on mutual transparency and unwavering dedication from both parties involved.

Step 2: User Interface and Experience Design

After completing the Inception phase, we commence our creative journey. Understanding that each client and their requirements are unique, our in-house UX/UI designer initiates by identifying valuable existing elements such as logos, typefaces, colors, and the current website. From there, we begin shaping and refining the visual brand that best represents your vision.

Step 3: Website Development

Development occurs concurrently in most cases, with the design team working on the HTML/CSS templates for all pages, views, modules and features of the website – while the development team begins building the database schema, framework and installation(s) that occur prior to design integration.
